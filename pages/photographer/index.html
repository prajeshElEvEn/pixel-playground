* IT'S ABOUT
ME AND HOW
I WORK

Hi, I'm Darrel Steward, a passionate photographer based in New York. I have always been captivated by the power of visual storytelling and photography allows me to freeze moments in time and convert emotions through images.

With a background in fine arts anf a keen eye for detail, each photography session as an opportunity to create art. Whether I'm capturing joy and connection of a family, the raw emotions of a wedding day, or the beauty of nature in landscape photography, my goal is to evoke a sense of wonder and create images that resonate with viewers.

I specialize in photography. Through my lens, I strive to capture the essence of my subjects, whether it's their unique personality, the ambiance of a specific location, or the interplay of light and shadow.
